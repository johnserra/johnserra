## Ingredients

- **1kg (2.2 lbs)** Yukon Gold or Russet potatoes
- **1 whole head** of garlic
- **60g (4 tbsp)** unsalted butter
- **60ml (1/4 cup)** high-quality Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- **120ml–180ml (1/2 to 3/4 cup)** warm milk or heavy cream
- **40g (1/2 cup)** grated Parmigiano-Reggiano or Pecorino Romano
- Salt and black pepper

---

## Method

1. **Roast Garlic:** Cut the top off the garlic head, drizzle with oil, wrap in foil. Roast at **200°C (400°F)** for 35 mins until soft.
2. **Boil Potatoes:** Peel and chunk potatoes. Boil in salted water until tender (15–20 mins). Drain and let steam for 2 mins.
3. **Mash:** Squeeze the roasted garlic cloves into the potatoes. Add butter and olive oil; mash until desired consistency is reached.
4. **Finish:** Gradually stir in warm milk/cream and fold in the grated cheese. Season to taste.

---

## Notes

The roasted garlic and olive oil give this mash a unique, savory flavor that pairs beautifully with rich, saucy dishes.
